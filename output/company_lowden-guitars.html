04Funnel Architecture▸

Acquisition: TOFU = Sheeran halo + NAMM trade press + YouTube dealer demos + IG craftsmanship reels. MOFU = lowdenguitars.com product pages + dealer locator. BOFU = direct online sales (most models) + worldwide authorized dealer network. Workshop tours (free, Fridays, max 6) ultra-high-intent.

Conversion Path: Hybrid DTC + dealer. Lowden states DTC orders do not jump dealer queues - both route into the same production batch. International shipping free. Lead times can be long (built-to-order).

Friction:

  • No financing - acknowledged FAQ gap
  • Lead-time uncertainty - built-to-order batches
  • Limited online customisation - email/chat required for bespoke options
  • Sheeran by Lowden funnel siloed from parent Lowden - no engineered cross-pollination

Channels:

  • DTC online (lowdenguitars.com)
  • Worldwide dealer network (Music Emporium, Wildwood, Eddie's, Heartbreaker, Acoustic Vibes, Distinctive, Guitar Center)
  • NAMM/trade show direct B2B
  • Workshop visits in Downpatrick

07Opportunities▸

Activate the 50-Year Founder Documentary as Always-On Content

THE GAPThe richest narrative asset in the company - George Lowden's 50-year personal arc - is documented in trade-press interviews but not serialised on owned channels.
WHAT WE DELIVER6-12 episode short-form series (IG/TikTok 90-second cuts + longer YouTube cuts) covering origin (1961 first guitar), Japanese era, 1988 receivership comeback, return from France, Sheeran origin, AI-sensor reinvention. Plus a 25-minute hero documentary.
Impact — Compounds organic reach on every artist mention; converts the slow-craft positioning from tagline into a daily-proof content engine; lifts DTC conversion.

Bridge the Sheeran Halo Back to the Parent Lowden Brand

THE GAP61K @sheeranguitars followers and Sheeran-attention spikes (Nov 2025 Belfast donation, Stadium Edition) under-monetise the parent brand because the funnels are siloed.
WHAT WE DELIVERDeliberate cross-funnel content matrix: every Sheeran-led story gets a paired piece on @lowdenguitars showing master-luthier provenance, plus shared CRM and a 'From Wee Lowden to Stadium Edition' content path walking aspirants up the Lowden ladder.
Impact — Captures aspirational Sheeran fans into higher-margin parent Lowden product line - directly addresses the demand-reduction problem MD Aaron Lowden identified.

Re-engineer the Funnel for Demand-Downturn Conditions

THE GAPFunnel leans on dealer demos + DTC quote-and-wait. With 'demand reduction' publicly acknowledged, Lowden needs warmer/faster mid-funnel.
WHAT WE DELIVER(a) Comparison/education content track ('Why Lowden vs Martin/Taylor/Collings') owned by Lowden, not dealers; (b) financing partnership creative (acknowledged gap); (c) creator/UGC partner programme with mid-tier acoustic creators on revenue share; (d) AI-sensor product story translated into 8-12 short videos.
Impact — Shortens buyer journey, converts the existing 121K combined IG audience, makes cost-cutting narrative compatible with growth.

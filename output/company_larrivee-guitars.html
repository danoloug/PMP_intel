MARKET SHARE

Larrivée Guitars (Jean Larrivée Guitars USA Inc.) is a family-run premium acoustic guitar manufacturer founded in Toronto in 1967 by Jean Larrivée. The company moved Toronto → Victoria (1977) → Vancouver (1982) → Oxnard, CA (2001) and consolidated all production in California in November 2013. The brand positions itself as 'affordable luxury' — all-solid-wood hand-built guitars priced 20-30 percent below comparable Taylor and Martin models. Jean still personally joins every top; wife Wendy designs the engravings; son Matthew is operations manager; son John Jr. is also active in the business.

07Opportunities▸

Own the Space Oddity halo

THE GAPChris Hadfield ISS parlor performance has 30M+ views and is one of the highest-reach guitar moments ever, but Larrivée's own channels rarely reactivate it. No anniversary cinematic, no follow-up, no behind-the-build cut.
WHAT WE DELIVERCinematic mini-documentary plus a serialised social campaign — 1 hero film, 6-8 short-form cuts, paid social to acoustic-guitar lookalike audiences, email arc for JCL Players Club.
Impact — Re-platforms a dormant cultural-moment asset into a measurable acquisition channel; aligns naturally with the 01 Series relaunch (parlor body is in the 01 lineup).

Convert the founder into a serialised content franchise

THE GAPJean Larrivée is documentary-grade subject matter (apprenticeship under Moench, walking BC log booms, multi-language wood-sourcing trips, 70+ still on the floor) and the company sits on archival footage from a 1983 CBC doc onward — none packaged as a recurring series.
WHAT WE DELIVER'Made by Larrivée' video series (6-12 episodes) — short cinematic dives into Jean obsessions (moonwood, X-brace, L-body origin). Cross-published to YouTube, IG Reels, JCL Club; licensed back to Destination Dealers for in-store display.
Impact — Builds a defensible content moat around the founder before he retires; gives Destination Dealers premium content competitors cannot match.

Reactivate the 01 Series + JCL Club as one creative arc

THE GAP01 Premier Series return for 2025 (from $1,299 — first entry tier in 25 years) is the biggest commercial event in a decade and is presented as a one-page product announcement. JCL Players Club (Sept 2025) and Destination Dealers (Nov 2025) launches are similarly siloed.
WHAT WE DELIVER2025-2026 integrated campaign — landing page, email arc into JCL, paid social to in-market acoustic buyers, 'your first Larrivée' UGC franchise, Destination Dealer co-op kit. A/B-tested creative variants from day one to establish the baseline Larrivée has never measured.
Impact — Turns three unrelated 2025 launches into one compounding funnel; establishes paid-social and A/B-testing muscle Larrivée currently lacks.
08Fit Assessment▸
▸ CURRENT STATE
  • Family-run ~25-person operation; most marketing handled internally and through dealer partnerships
  • Owned channels underleveraged: 23K IG, 96.5K FB, LarriveeMedia YouTube under-programmed
  • Dealer-led demo content dominates the visible long tail; brand controls less of its own creative output than it should
  • Three major 2025 launches (01 Series, Destination Dealers, JCL Club) run in parallel with no integrating campaign
  • No visible A/B testing, no measured paid-acquisition program, no first-party e-commerce attribution
▸ WHAT WE BRING
  • Cinematic founder-doc and serialised content production at a scale a 25-person family business cannot staff internally
  • Paid social + measurement architecture (creative testing, lookalike audiences, JCL email integration) to convert dormant brand equity into measurable demand
  • Campaign integration across the 01 Series relaunch, Destination Dealers expansion and JCL Players Club
  • Activation of the Chris Hadfield / Tommy Emmanuel / Robert Plant artist halo into structured creative — concert-friendly content rights and partnership flows
  • A creative-strategy operating system so the next launch is measured, not just announced
09Engagement Structure▸

Suggested Structure: Two-phase engagement: (1) a 4-6 week creative-strategy sprint producing the 2025-2026 integrated campaign framework, founder-series creative bible, and Space Oddity reactivation concept — paid as a fixed-fee discovery; (2) a 6-12 month execution retainer covering production of the founder series (3-4 episodes in the first quarter), the 01 Series launch campaign, paid-social testing, and monthly performance reporting.

Investment Framing: Frame against the commercial opportunity, not against Larrivée's prior marketing line items. The 01 Series relaunch alone — re-entry into a $1,299–1,500 acoustic price point Larrivée has been absent from for 25 years — is a multi-million-dollar revenue line if it lands. A six-figure annual marketing partnership is a small fraction of the upside if it adds even single-digit percentage points to 01 Series sell-through or to Destination Dealer same-store performance.

C.F. Martin and Co., founded 1833 by Christian Frederick Martin, is the oldest US guitar maker and the world's preeminent acoustic builder; family owned and operated for six generations, headquartered in Nazareth PA. Chris Martin IV is Executive Chairman; Thomas Ripsam is President and CEO.

MARKET POSITION Heritage leader; inventor of X-bracing and the Dreadnought; "the Stradivarius of guitars." Co-defines the premium acoustic market with Taylor.

McPherson Guitars is a high-end family-founded boutique acoustic guitar maker in Sparta, Wisconsin. Father-son duo Mander and Matt McPherson began the project in 1979, producing a three-soundhole design in Japan in the early 1980s. In 1995 Matt redesigned the line around his signature Offset Soundhole, Cantilevered Neck, and Overpass/Underpass bracing; since 2001 every McPherson guitar has been hand-built in Sparta. Founder Matt McPherson also founded and runs Mathews Archery, the world-leading compound-bow brand, also in Sparta — bow-tension physics directly informed the guitar designs.

06Storytelling Diagnostic▸

Current state: The brand tells an engineering story very well — offset soundhole, cantilevered neck, overpass/underpass bracing are explained consistently across surfaces. Premier Guitar and MMR coverage reinforce the master-luthier narrative. Reddit and dealer testimonials echo this.

▸ WORKING
  • Distinctive visual signature across every channel.
  • Engineering provenance establishes authority quickly.
  • High-quality founder/factory craft content where it exists.
▸ MISSING
  • Matt McPherson's wider story — multi-business entrepreneur leading the world's #1 archery brand, faith-driven design philosophy — barely referenced on McPherson Guitars surfaces.
  • Custom Shop journey (wood selection, inlays, 6-month build) has the thinnest creative footprint despite being highest-margin.
  • Artist endorser stories live on dealer or Facebook posts, not a flagship 'Played by' series.
  • Almost no emotional or aspirational customer-story narrative — storytelling is product- and process-led.
07Opportunities▸

Own the Custom Shop journey on video

THE GAPHighest-ASP, highest-margin product experience — 6-month bespoke build with wood selection, inlay design, direct shop collaboration — barely documented.
WHAT WE DELIVER6-12 episode 'Inside the Custom Shop' series following individual builds, plus paid Meta distribution to high-intent acoustic-guitar lookalikes.
Impact — Drives qualified custom-order leads to the $11K-$26K band where margin is highest and dealer commissions don't apply.

Build a flagship Artist Series content slate

THE GAPArtist endorser content fragmented across FB posts and dealer reviewers — no consolidated brand-owned 'Played by' showcase.
WHAT WE DELIVERMonthly 'Played by' release: one artist, one song, one story about choosing McPherson. Cut for YouTube long-form + IG Reels + Shorts.
Impact — Builds top-of-funnel reach, gives dealers shareable assets, creates compounding content library.

Activate the founder-as-character narrative

THE GAPMatt McPherson founded Mathews Archery (world's #1 compound bow) AND McPherson Guitars, applying bow-tension physics to acoustic design — one of the most distinctive entrepreneur stories in the instrument industry, invisible in marketing.
WHAT WE DELIVERFounder-led arc ('The Physics of Sound') connecting bow design to guitar design; faith-and-craft mini-documentary; paid amplification to acoustic + craftsmanship + outdoor-lifestyle audiences.
Impact — Differentiates against Martin/Taylor/Collings on identity rather than spec; opens lateral audiences (Mathews customers, outdoor/craft media) competitors cannot reach.

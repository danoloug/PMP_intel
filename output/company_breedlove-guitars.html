MARKET SHARE

Breedlove Guitars is a Bend, Oregon-based American acoustic guitar maker founded in 1990 by ex-Taylor luthiers Larry Breedlove and Steve Henderson. After two financial setbacks, the brand was acquired in 2010 by Tom Bedell's Two Old Hippies LLC, which led a sustainability-first reinvention. In June 2025 the company was sold again to Nashville-based musicians Pete Mroz (formerly NBC The Voice; business background at Ping Golf, Travis Mathew, FootJoy) and Shannon Pollard (founder of Plowboy Records, grandson of Eddy Arnold). At NAMM 2026 the new ownership launched a streamlined, USA-only lineup across three tiers: Roots, Artisan, and Collector — discontinuing all overseas production. The brand is one of the 'quartet of mid-size American custom-guitar manufacturers' alongside Bourgeois, Collings, and Santa Cruz.

07Opportunities▸

Cinematic Relaunch Story (replace press-release with a film)

THE GAPThe 2026 USA-only relaunch is being told via press release + dealer demos. No single owned cinematic asset explains WHY Pete and Shannon bought the company and WHY the line is now 100% Bend-built.
WHAT WE DELIVERA documentary-style 8–12 minute owned-channel film (plus 6–8 short-form cuts) profiling the owners, the Bend shop, the 2026 lineup, and the 'bringing it back home' decision.
Impact — Anchors the relaunch narrative for the next 24 months; gives dealers and press a single referenceable story; closes the long-standing 'What's a Breedlove?' recognition gap.

Short-form social engine + paid amplification

THE GAP@breedloveguitar (40K) and the YouTube channel publish episodically; no visible A/B testing; earned reach currently exceeds owned.
WHAT WE DELIVERA 90-day short-form program: weekly Reels/Shorts cuts mined from the factory, owner content, and artist features; structured A/B testing of hooks, CTAs, and audience segments; paid amplification to high-intent guitar-buyer audiences on Meta and YouTube.
Impact — Triples top-funnel reach without re-shooting; converts existing Bend-shop footage into a measurable demand-gen channel.

Owner-musician + Plowboy Records flywheel

THE GAPPete Mroz is a recording artist; Shannon Pollard founded Plowboy Records. Their personal platforms and label network are not integrated into Breedlove creative — a unique asset competitors cannot replicate.
WHAT WE DELIVERAn owner-led 'Played in Bend' artist series: Plowboy/Nashville artists recording on Breedlove instruments at the Bend shop, cross-posted from artist + brand channels. Anchored to the new Bend retail space (opening June 2026).
Impact — Defensible artist-relations program; converts music-industry credibility into compounding owned content; activates the new retail space as a content set, not just a store.

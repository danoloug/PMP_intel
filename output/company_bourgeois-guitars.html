PRICE BAND

Bourgeois Guitars, founded 1993 by master luthier Dana Bourgeois at the Roy Continental Mill (later Roy Hill Mill, 41 Canal St) in Lewiston, Maine, is a boutique builder of premium acoustic guitars. Pantheon Guitars formed in 2000 with investors after a 1999 bankruptcy; October 2019 strategic partnership with Eastman Music for international distribution; Dana remains owner and CEO.

MARKET POSITIONPremium boutique acoustic luthier; founder/voicing authority; product lines Legacy, Heirloom, Aged Tone, Professional, Touchstone, Downeast, Custom Shop, Mandolins

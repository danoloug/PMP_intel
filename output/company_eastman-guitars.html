MARKET SHARE

Eastman Guitars is the guitar division of Eastman Music Company, a privately-held musical instrument maker founded in 1992 by Qian Ni, a Beijing-born flutist who started the business out of the back of his car after graduating from Boston University School of Music. The company began as an importer/maker of violins, violas and cellos using traditional 19th-century European workshop methods, then progressively expanded into archtop guitars and mandolins (2002), steel-string acoustic flat-tops (2008), thinline electrics (2012), and solidbody electrics (2016). Parent Eastman Music Company is now a multi-brand house that includes Eastman Strings, Eastman Winds, Wm. S. Haynes (flutes, est. 1888), S.E. Shires, Backun Musical Services and (since 2019) Bourgeois Guitars. Guitars are hand-built in Beijing workshops using primarily hand tools and a few band saws; final inspection and setup happens in San Luis Obispo / Pomona, California. Eastman occupies a distinctive market niche: handcrafted, all-solid-wood instruments at price points well below comparable Martin/Gibson/Collings builds, positioned as a serious alternative for working professionals and high-end hobbyists.

04Funnel Architecture▸

Acquisition: Top-of-funnel is driven by: (1) press reviews in major guitar media (Guitar World, MusicRadar, Premier Guitar, Guitarist magazine), (2) dealer demo videos on YouTube, (3) NAMM trade-show debuts, (4) artist endorsement social posts, (5) #iplayeastman community UGC, (6) word-of-mouth on guitar forums (Reddit r/Guitar, r/EastmanGuitarOwners, AGF, UMGF).

Conversion Path: Eastman runs a pure dealer-network funnel - no DTC checkout on eastmanguitars.com. Site offers product detail pages, FAQ, and a dealer locator (US + Europe). Customers convert at authorized dealers either in-store or via dealer e-commerce (Sweetwater, Chicago Music Exchange, The Acoustic Shoppe, Music Zoo).

Friction:

  • No DTC means Eastman cannot capture first-party purchase data, customer email at purchase, or remarketing audiences
  • Dealer locator is functional but discovery-heavy - customers must drive deeper into the funnel before seeing inventory
  • Newsletter signup is the only first-party lead capture on the site - underutilized
  • No discoverable lifecycle/email nurture program for prospects who don't convert immediately

Channels:

  • Wholesale to authorized dealers (primary)
  • Dealer e-commerce (secondary)
  • Sub-brand Bourgeois distributed through Eastman internationally
  • No direct-to-consumer storefront

07Opportunities▸

Beijing Workshop Documentary Series

THE GAPEastman's biggest differentiator (hand-built in a 19th-century-style violin workshop in Beijing) has no first-party cinematic creative. Press and dealers describe it; Eastman doesn't show it.
WHAT WE DELIVERA 3-5 episode mini-doc series filmed in the Beijing workshop: master luthiers at their benches, hand-scalloped bracing, the antique-varnish process, the tap-tuning ritual. 1 hero film + cut-downs for IG Reels, YouTube Shorts, paid-social hooks.
Impact — Owns the differentiator narrative; creates a year of evergreen creative; doubles or triples qualified top-of-funnel traffic to dealers; enables Eastman to compete on story (not just price) against Martin/Gibson/Collings.

Founder + Artist Story Series

THE GAPQian Ni's immigrant-luthier journey and the working-artist roster (Stroud, Santo, Wilson, Vignola, Tubbs) are static assets on the site. They could be the most-shared content in the category.
WHAT WE DELIVEREpisodic 'Why I Play Eastman' artist-portrait series (3-5 min each) + a founder origin film. Bundled with a redesigned artists page, podcast-style audio cuts, and a quarterly content calendar.
Impact — Converts a static endorsement program into ongoing PR, social, and SEO equity; gives dealers and press a steady flow of co-marketable content; builds the lifecycle nurture asset Eastman currently lacks.

DTC Lead-Capture + Lifecycle Engine

THE GAPNo DTC checkout means no first-party purchase data, but the site captures only a passive newsletter signup. Customers visit, browse, leave, and Eastman has nothing to remarket to.
WHAT WE DELIVERLead-capture redesign (gated dealer-locator, model-comparison wizard, 'build my Eastman' configurator email), a 6-touch nurture journey, and a CRM segmentation model that hands warm leads to specific dealers. Paired with a Meta/YouTube paid layer once owned content is in place.
Impact — Creates the missing middle of the funnel; gives Eastman a measurable acquisition channel for the first time; protects dealer relationships while adding brand-level demand signal.
